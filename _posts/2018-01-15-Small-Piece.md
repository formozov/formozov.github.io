### Just a Small Piece of “Philosophy”

I would like to provide two fundamental statements (so far without any explanations):

- Artificial Intellegence (AI) will be created (Sure).
- Brain-Computer Interface (BCI) will be created (Probably).

You can see that the situation for AI and BCI  is obviously not symmetrical. 
My aim is somehow increase the probability of the BCI creation, before the first appearance of AI.
